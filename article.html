Main Idea 1 “What is global warming”

the science of global warming

Greenhouse gasses

Global Warming is the rise of average temperature around the globe. Global warming is mainly caused by our increased reliance on Greenhouse gases in the last couple decades.

Greenhouse gases are gases that trap solar radiation.

These are the most 6 abundant Greenhouse gases (in order):

  1. Water Vapor
  2. Carbon dioxide
  3. Methane
  4. Nitrous oxide
  5. Ozone
  6. CFCs

Since the Industrial Revolution[1] we have rapidly increased our use and dependence on fossil fuels. Fussil fuels emit large amounts of carbon dioxide, this creates more Greenhouse gases.

The Greenhouse effect is the idea that the suns rays are hitting the earth, then bouncing off. Now you’d think that the bouncing rays would go right up and away, but instead, Greenhouse gases are partially trapping the heat in.

The problem is that we are making to much Greenhouse gases, so much that a lot more of the heat is staying in. The consequences are scary, imagine us one day boiling alive.
